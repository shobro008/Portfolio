Procedure for Onboarding

  1. Registration through the Portal www.NITKKR.com OR www.Dr.BRNITJDR.com
  2. ID login and authentication
  3. Submission of the Project on the Portal
    • Open the tab Submit Proposal
      • The Tab is Under More Glance tab
      • Click the Student Contribution Section
      • Upload and Press Submit
    • Select your Project
    • Click Upload the Project Button
  4. Result on Registered Email a week later
